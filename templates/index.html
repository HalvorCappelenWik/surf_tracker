🧭 New to Nova? Where to Surf

  • 🏄 Ericeira: Nova’s weekend favorite. Chill town, consistent swell.
  • 🌊 Carcavelos: Easy train ride, popular after class.
  • 🌪 Nazaré: Great to watch — not to surf (unless you’re a superhero).

🏄‍♂️ Surfboard Rentals & Lessons

  • Boardriders Ericeira – boards, wetsuits, and lessons (10% student discount!)
  • Carcavelos Surf School – great for quick after-class sessions
  • Magic Quiver – premium boards in Ericeira

💡 Many surf schools offer discounts if you show your Nova student card!
